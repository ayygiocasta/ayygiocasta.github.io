JAM Project - 未来への咆哮 / Asu e no Houkou
(Muv-Luv Alternative OP)

Since the original guitar parts were obviously written to provide a rhythmic/harmonic bed for the vocals, I changed a few parts to sound better in a mix where the guitar is so loud compared to everything else, just a couple of arpeggios and flat 5 dyads and the second half of the chorus.
