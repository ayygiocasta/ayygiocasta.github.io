Translations


I started doing little JP->EN translations for fun.
My japanese is by no means good (in fact, it's basically non-existent) and I honestly have no idea how accurate these are, or even how much am I allowed to twist the phrasing and sentence structure to make it sound more natural and less robotic compared to a word-by-word translation.

Since I'm borderline autistic I'm also translating the SFXs/onomatopoeias and doing all the cleaning/redrawing work, yes, I know you're only supposed to do that when translating manga, I'm incredibly sorry.
Oh, and I usually try to use a font as close as possible to the one in the original picture, but I might spice it up a bit, if I'm feeling particularly creative.

If multiple images are part of the same story, the reading order is right to left.

Also, I know this kind of layout is absolutely sub-optimal for browsing pictures, but I'd rather have this than make an interactive gallery with j***script, sorry.
